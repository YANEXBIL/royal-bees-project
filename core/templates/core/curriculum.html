ROYAL BEES SCHOOLS offers a comprehensive curriculum based on the Nigerian National Policy on Education, enriched with international best practices to provide a globally competitive education. Our curriculum is designed to be broad, balanced, and relevant to the needs of our students.

Levels of Education:

  • Creche/Pre-Nursery: Focus on early childhood development, play-based learning.
  • Nursery (KG Classes): Numeracy, Literacy, Phonics, Creative Arts, Social Habits.
  • Primary (Basic 1-6): Core subjects including English Studies, Mathematics, Basic Science & Technology (BST), National Values Education (NVE), Pre-Vocational Studies (PVS), Cultural & Creative Arts (CCA), Nigerian Languages (Yoruba/Igbo/Hausa as applicable), French.
  • Junior Secondary (JSS 1-3 / Basic 7-9): Building on primary education with a wider range of subjects leading to the Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE).
  • Senior Secondary (SSS 1-3): Specialization in Science, Arts, or Commercial subjects, preparing students for WASSCE, NECO SSCE, and international examinations like IGCSE (if applicable).

Subject Areas (Example for Secondary):

While specific subjects vary by level and stream, core areas include:

  • English Language & Literature-in-English
  • Mathematics & Further Mathematics
  • Sciences: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Agricultural Science
  • Languages: French, Nigerian Languages
  • Humanities: Government, History, Geography, Economics, Civic Education
  • Technology: Basic Technology, ICT/Computer Studies, Technical Drawing
  • Vocational/Commercial: Business Studies, Accounting, Home Economics, Visual Arts
