Admission Process

  1. Obtain Application Form: Application forms can be obtained from the school's administrative office or downloaded from our website (link coming soon).
  2. Submit Application: Completed application forms along with all required documents should be submitted to the admissions office by the specified deadline.
  3. Entrance Assessment: Prospective students will be required to undertake an age-appropriate entrance assessment/interview. Dates for assessments will be communicated upon submission of the application.
  4. Offer of Admission: Successful candidates will receive an offer of admission.
  5. Acceptance & Payment: Acceptance of the admission offer must be confirmed by paying the required fees by the due date.

  • Completed Application Form
  • Photocopy of Candidate's Birth Certificate

  • Photocopy of Candidate's most recent Academic Report/Transcript from previous school
